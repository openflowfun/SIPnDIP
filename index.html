🌾

Crunchy Granola Base

House-made granola with caramelised nuts — the satisfying crunch in every spoonful.

🍫

Rich Belgian Chocolate Sauce

Premium Belgian chocolate poured warm, pooling into a silky, indulgent layer.

🍓

Fresh NZ Strawberries

Locally sourced, picked-fresh strawberries. Sweet, fragrant, and never frozen.

SIP N DIP is Christchurch's authentic Açaí & Dessert Bar — born from a love of premium, photogenic desserts inspired by the viral Dubai Pistachio trend.

Every cup is handcrafted to order, layering the finest local NZ ingredients with Middle-Eastern-inspired flavours into cups as stunning to look at as they are to eat.
