Our Story

New Zealand's Most
Instagram-Worthy Dessert

SIP N DIP is New Zealand's premium Açaí & Dessert Bar — born from a love of photogenic, Dubai-inspired desserts that taste as incredible as they look.

What started in Christchurch has grown nationwide, now serving across Auckland and Tauranga too. Every cup is handcrafted to order, layering NZ-fresh ingredients with Middle-Eastern-inspired flavours.
